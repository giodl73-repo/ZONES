# Civil Time Policy Reviewer

AI-simulated review role for civil-time policy scope.

## Reviews

- Whether ZONES distinguishes measurement from recommendation.
- Whether legal authority and enactment language are caveated.
- Whether current and proposed time-zone boundaries are described neutrally.
- Whether daylight saving time assumptions are explicit.
- Whether active legislation and current federal constraints are separated from
  design scenarios.

## Blocks

- Claims that a map is legally valid or should be enacted without review.
- Operational scheduling advice.
- Hidden normative weights presented as objective truth.
- Recommendation language before source, score, and publication review.
