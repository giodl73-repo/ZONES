# Track E - Global And Historical Systems

Papers in this track:

- E.0+global-zone-systems
- E.1+historical-zone-change
- E.2+country-boundary-pilots

## Module Question

Can the ZONES model represent time-zone boundaries and offset rules across
countries and historical periods without pretending that one source is complete
legal truth?

## Hypothesis Gate

Before drafting, each paper must pass a `/researchprewrite`-style check:

- state the country or historical period being tested,
- name boundary and time-rule sources,
- define source limitations and missing-law caveats,
- specify validity intervals and regime authority,
- define what source conflict would limit or falsify the comparison.
